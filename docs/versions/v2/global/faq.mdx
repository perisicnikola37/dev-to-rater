Here are some common questions and their answers to help you get started with **Dev.to Rater**.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Dev.to Rater?">
    <p>**Dev.to Rater** is a tool that helps you analyze your posts on Dev.to by uncovering trends, engagement metrics, and content patterns. It provides actionable insights to optimize your posts for better engagement and a wider audience.</p>
  </Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I contribute to the project?">
  <p>
    Yes! We welcome contributions. You can submit pull requests, improve the
    documentation, report bugs, or suggest new features. We encourage you to
    follow best practices and maintain high code quality.
  </p>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What should I do if the app crashes or doesn’t work?">
  <p>
    If the app crashes or isn’t working as expected, check the error messages in
    the logs and ensure all dependencies are installed. You can also check the
    Node.js version compatibility. If the issue persists, please report it on
    our [GitHub Issues
    page](https://github.com/perisicnikola37/dev-to-rater/issues).
  </p>
</Accordion>

    <Accordion title="How can I contact the team?">
    <p>If you have any questions, feedback, or need assistance, feel free to reach out to us. You can either:</p>
    <ul>
      <li>Open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/perisicnikola37/dev-to-rater/issues).</li>
      <li>You can also reach us via email at: devtorater@gmail.com.</li>
    </ul>

  </Accordion>

   <Accordion title="Is Dev.to Rater free to use?">
    <p>Yes! **Dev.to Rater** is completely free to use. We believe in open-source and providing value to the community without any cost. Feel free to use it, and let us know if you have any suggestions for improvement!</p>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

> If you have questions, feel free to reach out or open an issue on our [GitHub page](https://github.com/perisicnikola37/dev-to-rater/issues).
